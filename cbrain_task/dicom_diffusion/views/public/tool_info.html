Dicom diffusion pipeline

The dicom diffusion pipeline processes dicom diffusion MRI data and outputs various DTI and HARDI metrics.

    Step by step procedure:
  • Dicom conversion to nifti (via MRTrix's mrconvert)
  • Gradient direction and intensity extraction (via MRTrix's mrinfo)
  • Brain extraction (via FSL bet)
  • Brain extraction (via FSL bet)
  • Denoising with the NLMeans alorithm
  • DTI metrics computation
  • CSA reconstruction
  • CSD reconstruction
